NgKore Community Meetings

NGKORE FOUNDATION organises structured community meetings to drive open collaboration, working-group focus, and technical leadership across Quantum Security, AI/ML, and Telecom.

How NgKore Meetings Work

NgKore meetings are designed to be predictable, inclusive, and action-oriented. Working Groups (WGs) meet biweekly to focus on a specific technical domain, while the Technical Advisory Committee (TAC) meets monthly to guide the overall direction and functioning of NGKORE FOUNDATION.

  • Working Group Meetings (Biweekly) – Dedicated spaces for deep–dive technical collaboration in Quantum Security, AI/ML, and Telecom.
  • Technical Advisory Committee (Monthly) – A governance and strategy–focused call covering roadmap, ecosystem partnerships, and open community priorities.

All times are listed in Indian Standard Time (IST, UTC +5:30).

You can also subscribe to all NgKore meetings via NgKore Calendar and import them into your preferred calendar using the provided .ics files.
